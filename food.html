Famous Street Foods of Mumbai

Vada Pav

Spicy potato filling in a bun, Mumbai’s classic snack.

Pani Puri

Crispy puris filled with spicy and tangy water.

Bhel Puri

A mix of puffed rice, veggies, and chutneys.

Misal Pav

Spicy curry with sprouts, served with pav.

Pav Bhaji

Buttery pav with spicy mashed veggies.

Sev Puri

Chaat with crispy puris, sev, chutneys, and veggies.

Kheema Pav

Minced meat curry served with soft buns.

Bombay Sandwich

Layered veg sandwich with chutney and masala.

Falooda

A sweet dessert drink with rose syrup, milk, and jelly.

Modak

Sweet dumplings filled with coconut and jaggery.
